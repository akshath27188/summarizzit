Summarizzit - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Key Points:
  • We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service
  • Post content is sent to AI providers (Google, OpenAI, Perplexity) for summarization
  • We do NOT sell your data or use it for advertising
  • Summaries are cached temporarily to improve performance

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Summarizzit ("we", "us", "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Reddit app. By using Summarizzit, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Reddit User ID: Your unique Reddit user identifier (e.g., "t2_xxxxx") for quota tracking and payment verification
  • Usage Data: Number of summaries generated, timestamps, success/failure rates
  • Technical Data: Subreddit context, post IDs you summarize
  • Payment Status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription (verified through Reddit's API)

2.2 User-Provided Content

  • Reddit Post URLs: Links to posts you request to summarize
  • Post Content: Titles, body text, and up to 25 top comments from requested posts

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Reddit password or authentication credentials
  • Payment card information (handled by Reddit)
  • Private messages or inbox content
  • Your voting history or saved posts
  • Personal information beyond your Reddit user ID
  • Browser fingerprints or tracking cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Generate AI summaries of Reddit posts you request
  • Quota Management: Track free tier usage (10 summaries/month) and enforce limits
  • Payment Verification: Check if you have an active Pro subscription
  • Caching: Store summaries temporarily to improve performance and reduce costs
  • Analytics: Monitor API costs, error rates, and service health
  • Service Improvement: Identify bugs, optimize performance, and enhance features

4. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

4.1 AI Processing Services

To generate summaries, we send post content and comments to the following AI providers:

  • Google Gemini AI (primary provider)
  • OpenAI GPT models (fallback provider)
  • Perplexity AI (fallback provider)

Data Sent: Post title, body, and up to 25 comments (truncated to 200 characters each)

Data Retention by AI Providers: These services may retain data according to their own policies. We recommend reviewing:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Perplexity Privacy Policy

4.2 Reddit Platform

We interact with Reddit's API to:

  • Fetch post content and comments
  • Verify your payment status
  • Identify your user ID

Reddit's Privacy Policy governs their data handling.

4.3 Data We Do NOT Share

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Provide data to data brokers
  • Use your content for training our own AI models

5. Data Storage & Retention

5.1 Cached Summaries

  • Storage Location: Redis database (managed by Reddit/Devvit)
  • Retention Period: Up to 30 days
  • Purpose: Avoid regenerating summaries for the same post, reducing costs and improving speed

5.2 Usage Metrics

  • Retention Period: 90 days
  • Data Stored: Summary count per user per month, API costs, error logs

5.3 Payment Records

  • Retention Period: 35 days (for subscription verification)
  • Data Stored: Order IDs, subscription status, timestamps

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • API keys are stored securely in Reddit's settings system
  • Data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Redis database access is restricted to the App
  • No passwords or sensitive credentials are stored

However: No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
